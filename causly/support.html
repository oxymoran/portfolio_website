Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?
Causly is a "local-first" app. All your data lives right on your device. We do not have servers that store your personal health information.

How do I back up my data?
Go to Settings > Data Management. You can export a backup file which you can save to your iCloud, Google Drive, or email to yourself.

I submitted a log by mistake. Can I delete it?
Yes. Go to the Journal tab, find the day with the incorrect log, tap "Edit" on the entry, and you will find a delete option.
