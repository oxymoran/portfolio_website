Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

Your privacy is the highest priority for Causly. This Privacy Policy describes how Causly ("we", "us", "our") handles your data to ensure complete transparency and trust.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Causly is a "local-first" application. This means we prioritize your device over any cloud-based storage.

  • All health data, symptom logs, and personal notes you enter are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not transmit your health data to our servers.
  • We do not have a centralized database containing your personal health information.

2. HealthKit & Health Connect

Causly integrates with Apple HealthKit (iOS) and Health Connect (Android) to provide a comprehensive view of your health patterns.

  • Data Accessed: We may read data such as Sleep duration, Heart Rate, Steps, and Activity levels.
  • Usage: This data is used solely to correlate lifestyle factors (like sleep quality) with your logged symptom severity, helping you understand your unique health triggers.
  • Sharing: We do not share your health data with third parties, advertisers, or any other entities.

3. Data Deletion

You have full and absolute control over your data:

  • Manual Deletion: You can delete individual logs or trackers at any time within the app.
  • Full Reset: You can use the "Delete All Data" button in the Settings menu to permanently erase all locally stored data from your device.
  • App Uninstall: Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data (unless you have manually exported a backup file).

4. Backups

Causly allows you to export your data for backup purposes. These files are stored on your device or your chosen cloud storage (e.g., iCloud, Google Drive). We do not control, access, or have visibility into these backup files.
