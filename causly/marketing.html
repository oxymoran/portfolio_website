A private, local-first symptom journal designed to help you connect the dots between your lifestyle, environment, and wellbeing.

Why Causly?

Your health is complex. Tracking it shouldn't be.

  • Private & Secure: Your data never leaves your device. No cloud servers, no data selling.
  • Custom Trackers: Create trackers for anything—migraines, allergies, mood, or medication.
  • Health Integration: Automatically syncs sleep, exercise, and heart rate data from Apple Health & Health Connect.
  • Weather Insights: Automatically logs local weather, AQI, and pollen to help identify environmental triggers.
